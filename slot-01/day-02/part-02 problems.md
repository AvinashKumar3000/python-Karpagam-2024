## problem 03

    Given a series 12 + 32 + 52 + 72 + . . . + (2*n – 1)2, 
    find sum of the series.
    Examples:
    Input : n = 4
    Output : 84
    Explanation :
    sum = 12 + 32 + 52 + 72
        = 1 + 9 + 25 + 49
        = 84
    Input : n = 10
    Output : 1330
    Explanation :
    sum = 12 + 32 + 52 + 72 + 92 + 112 + 132 + 152 + 172 + 192
        = 1 + 9 + 24 + 49 + . . . + 361
        = 1330
